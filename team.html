Free Movie Viewing Activities

Activity Introduction

NEWHS collaborates with major film companies worldwide, employing a unique method where members watch movie trailers to earn income and promote the films. The goal is to distribute and spread free movie trailers, attracting a large potential audience, increasing the films' visibility and anticipation, and ultimately building a strong box office and reputation for the official release. You only need to successfully form a group with friends to get your VIP deposit refunded. This way, both the film companies and NEWHS members can profit together, enabling everyone to participate and earn money by watching trailers from the comfort of their homes.
